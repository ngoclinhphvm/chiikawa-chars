Chiikawa: Small and Cute

Chiikawa (ちいかわ), also known as Nanka Chiisakute Kawaii Yatsu (なんか小さくてかわいいやつ, "Something Small and Cute"), is a Japanese manga series by Nagano. It has been serialized online via Twitter since January 2020 and has been collected in six tankōbon volumes by Kodansha. An anime television series adaptation by Doga Kobo premiered in April 2022.

Main Characters

Chiikawa. A little critter who lives in a big house. Small, cute and easily moved to tears, shy and scared of every monster they meet. Yet always try to be stronger, and be a good friend.

Hachiware. Looks like a cat but is not one. Chatty, likes to talk and sing. Can talk and communicate to others. Kind-hearted and willing to help friends. Sometimes innocent or unaware in dangerous situations.

Usagi. Looks like a rabbit but is not one. Same language as Chiikawa's. Tends to shout rather than speak quietly. Intelligent, positive. Obtained a level 3 weeding certification.

Kurimanju. Looks cute, but acts like a middle-aged Japanese man who loves alcohol. Often exhales a spirited “Haah!” when consuming delicious alcohol or food. A little intimidating, yet has a kind side, surprisingly taking good care of others.

Momonga. Fluffy appearance. Too unrestrained personality. Persistently bothers someone or insists on their whims, causing almost 100% confusing situations and becoming the cause of trouble.

Shisa. A gourmet report character that eats food and earnestly teaches the taste. Admires and calls the master of the ramen shop, Rou 'Master'. Currently studying to obtain a liquor qualification.

Rakko. A brave otter character, whose bravery and behavior attract admiration and respect from many characters. Has a cross-shaped scar on the forehead. Usually acts alone. Can speak fluently, and communicate with other characters.

Pajama Parties. A group that sings and dances while wearing the pajamas the Pochette Yoroi-san made.
